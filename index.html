PDF to Text

This online tool allows you to extract text from a PDF file. It removes non-standard characters and extra spaces, providing a more readable text output. You can download the extracted text to a (.txt) text file or copy it to your clipboard for further use. Your PDF file is processed locally on your device by your browser using Mozilla PDF.js, and no data is transferred or stored elsewhere.
