PDF to Text

Client side browser tool to extract text from a PDF file using PDF.js

Some cleanup will be performed on the extracted text including removing non-standard characters and extra spaces to provide a more readable output. You can download the extracted text to a (.txt) text file or copy it to your clipboard for further use. Your PDF file is processed locally on your device by your browser using Mozilla PDF.js, and no data is transferred or stored elsewhere.
